IMPORTANT

Due to the high workload related to the COVID-19 pandemic, the Unemployment Insurance program is allowing customers to send one message per day. The EDD will respond to your message in the order received. Thank you for your patience.

The EDD will process your request to backdate the effective date of your new UI claim (or new effective reopen date) in the order received. You will receive a Notice of Amended Unemployment Insurance Award, DE 429 by mail. Continue to certify for weekly benefits if you are still unemployed or working part-time.

In the space below:

  1. Provide the new effective date you are requesting to backdate your UI claim (or new effective reopen date) with the date format “MM/DD/YYYY.”
  2. Type, “Backdate my claim to MM/DD/YYYY.”
